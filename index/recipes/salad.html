Ceaser Salad

Caesar salad is a classic and popular dish known for its crisp and fresh combination of ingredients. It typically consists of crisp romaine lettuce leaves tossed with Caesar dressing, which is a rich and creamy dressing made with ingredients like olive oil, garlic, Dijon mustard, anchovies, Parmesan cheese, and lemon juice. The salad is often topped with croutons for a crunchy texture and grated Parmesan cheese for added flavor. Some variations may include grilled chicken or bacon for additional protein and taste. The result is a satisfying salad that balances the creaminess of the dressing with the crispness of the lettuce and the savory notes from Parmesan and anchovies.

Ingredients

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Ceaser Dressing
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan Cheese (grated)
  • optional: protiens such as grilled chicken.

    • Steps

    1. Wash and Chop Lettuce: Clean and chop fresh Romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
    2. Prepare Caesar Dressing: Mix together olive oil, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, anchovies, grated Parmesan cheese, and fresh lemon juice to create the Caesar dressing.
    3. Toss Lettuce with Dressing: In a large bowl, toss the chopped lettuce with the prepared Caesar dressing until evenly coated.
    4. Add Croutons: Gently fold in croutons to the salad for a crunchy texture.
    5. Grate Parmesan Cheese: Grate additional Parmesan cheese over the salad for added flavor.
    6. Optional Additions: If desired, add grilled chicken or bacon for extra protein and taste.
    7. Serve: Plate the Caesar salad and serve immediately as a refreshing and flavorful dish.
